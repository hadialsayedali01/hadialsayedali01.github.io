Ottawa Vehicular Accident Analysis 2017-2022

An analysis of vehicular accidents in Ottawa from 2017 to 2022 that uses Python's data science libraries. The project identifies and visualizes correlations between collision severity/frequency and factors such as traffic control, locations, and environmental conditions. It also contextualizes the periods of time where the most severe and frequent accidents occurred. Lastly, I optimize and apply three machine learning models to predict an accident's severity based on the distribution of its participants.
